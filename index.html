The Brief

Who is this for?

An active, influential storyteller with an interesting story to tell.

Someone who's interested in using new technology to bring their story to life. They want to tell it beautifully. They want to share it with the world.

Writers Filmmakers Journalists Founders Educators Artists

We invite them to be one of the special 100 — and give them not just the tool, but the platform.

A chance to be immortalised in the history of novel storytelling.
